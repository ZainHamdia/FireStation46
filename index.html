Support Station 46

Montgomery Township Volunteer Fire Company #2 is an all-volunteer, 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Your generous donations help us continue to protect lives and property.

Your Support Saves Lives

Unlike career fire departments, we rely on the generosity of our community members, businesses, and municipal support to fund our daily operations and equipment. Your contributions directly fund:

  • Protective Gear: Outfitting a single volunteer firefighter with bunker gear, helmet, boots, and SCBA respirator costs over $10,000.
  • Rescue Tools: Thermal cameras, gas meters, hydraulic vehicle extrication tools, and swift water rescue equipment.
  • Continuous Training: Regular drills, fire academy certification courses, and specialized rescue training.
