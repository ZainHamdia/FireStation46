Standard Membership

  • • 100% Volunteer Fire Department. Our members are students, retirees, parents, teachers, business owners, healthcare professionals, and more—all dedicated to serving the community.
  • • No experience needed. We provide all training and equipment at no cost.
  • • Learn valuable skills through hands-on training at the Somerset County Emergency Services Training Academy and alongside experienced firefighters.
  • • Firefighting is about teamwork, problem-solving, and a willingness to learn—not just physical strength.
  • • Multiple ways to serve:
    • Interior Firefighter
    • Traffic Control / Fire Police
    • Associate Member (fundraising, events, equipment and building maintenance, and other support roles)
  • • Open to anyone 18 and up. We welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels.
  • • Make a difference, build lifelong friendships, and give back to your community.

Junior Firefighter Program

  • Open to ages 16–18 interested in learning about the fire service.
  • Respond to emergency calls (within program guidelines), attend training, meetings, and fire company events.
  • Train alongside experienced firefighters and gain hands-on experience in skills such as:
    • Vehicle extrication
    • Brush fires
    • Radio communications
    • SCBA (air packs)
    • Rescue techniques
  • Free Firefighter 1 certification through the Somerset County Emergency Services Training Academy (SCESTA) after your 18th birthday.
  • School and family come first. Junior Firefighters are never expected to miss school or academic activities.
